Office Administrator (P/T)

We’re looking to recruit a highly organised and driven Office Administrator as part of our Client Services team. The role will be 4-days a week Monday-Thursday and will be office based in our studio in Horwich. We would also be open to applicants who wish to work reduced hours across these days.

Absolute is an integrated digital agency with bold ideas, specialising in brand building, digital innovation and campaign delivery. Our studio is based in a 10,000 sq ft converted church in Horwich, close to the M61 motorway (30 minutes from Manchester and Preston) and Horwich Parkway train station with free parking outside the studio.

The Role:

You will be responsible for undertaking general administrative duties within the agency and supporting our agency staff. You would be responsible for the following tasks:

  • Managing company correspondence, including phone calls, emails, letters and packages

  • First point of contact for our visiting Clients

  • Co-ordinating client meetings and refreshments

  • Co-ordinating client meetings and refreshments

  • Ordering office supplies such as stationary and consumables

  • Managing staff holiday requests

  • Making travel arrangements

  • Managing meeting room bookings

  • Data entry and maintaining agency records

  • Organising internal and external events

  • Supporting the Client Services team and other agency staff with various administrative tasks

Experience:

The ideal candidate will have the following attributes:

  • 2-3 years experience in a similar role

  • Great organisational skills

  • Multitasking abilities

  • Strong verbal communication skills

  • Good writing skills

  • Strong typing skills

  • Time management skills

  • Excellent interpersonal skills

  • Project management skills

Nice to Have:

To have previous experience in a variety of basic computer programs you would expect in an office environment. These include:

  • Excel spreadsheets

  • Outlook

  • MS Office (Microsoft Office)

  • PowerPoint

  • Social Media platforms

  • Google calendars

  • Zoom/Teams/Google meet programs

A great studio environment
We’ve invested a great deal in our studio space, and this was included in Prolific North's "Most Inspiring Places to Work" campaign a few years ago. This includes communal spaces to break away from your desk, work in project specific studios etc. We also have our own ping-pong and pool table areas along with a new studio bar to let off steam.

Team building activities
We like to get together and socialise as often as we can. Team lunches, quiz nights, nights out and even trips abroad are all part and parcel of working with Absolute. We’ve even hired chefs to come in and cook amazing meals for the team in our studio.

Health is a priority
We’re aware that we don’t work in the most active of jobs, which is why we’ve built our own in-house gym for all staff to keep you happy and healthy. This includes a full subscription to Peloton (Bike & Running machine). We also keep our studio stocked with fresh fruit every week alongside access to mental well-being services should you need it. Also, being based very close to the picturesque Rivington, we’ve invested in Absolute Mountain Bikes for you to explore the reservoirs or hills around the agency to clear your head.
