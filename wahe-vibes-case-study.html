Shopify Website Creation for Wahe Vibes

Wahe Vibes, a holistic herbal tea brand, entrusted us with the creation of their Shopify website. Rooted in the founder Jitty's spiritual journey, we designed a platform that radiates tranquility and health through a harmonious blend of green and beige tones. The site is a gateway to a serene world, echoing the brand's commitment to well-being and Ayurveda.
